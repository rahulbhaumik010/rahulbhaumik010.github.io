About Me

I am a designer and researcher exploring design thinking, human-computer interaction (HCI), extended reality (XR), design pedagogy, and design for learning.

Research

I am currently focusing on the design and development of novel in-car interfaces for improved user experience. My work centers around textile-based interfaces, with a specific focus on creating a 3-dimensional, touch-sensitive middle console for cars.

Experience

  • Researcher (PhD Scholar) – Free University of Bozen Bolzano (2023 - present)
  • Assistant Professor – Faculty of Architecture & Design, PES University, Bangalore (2018 - 2023)

Projects
